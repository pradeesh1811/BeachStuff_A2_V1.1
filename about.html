A Little Story About Us

This whole adventure started in 2012 with a tiny shop lovingly built with the help of family
& friends in an amazing community. After over a decade of working in the fashion industry I
decided to leave the city and look for a more meaningful lifestyle. California and it's waves
have stolen my heart and I've never looked back! Everything we carry in the shop represents
my interpretation of the Golden State. Sun, beaches, parties, and nostalgic - things
you might find at a party city.
